Psychologists often notice poor abstract thinking. Extremely low IQ individuals struggle with. Sarcasm, metaphor, nuance, implied meaning. They understand words, but misinterpreted. Psychology links this to weak pattern recognition. Low IQ minds struggle to connect cause and effect over time. They experience life as random instead of predictable.
