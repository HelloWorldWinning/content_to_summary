**Summary:**

**I. NPD (Narcissistic Personality Disorder) in Marriage:**
    *   **A. Selfishness and Self-Preservation:**
        *   NPD individuals prioritize their own needs above all else.
        *   They disregard the needs and feelings of their spouse.
        *   They perceive their spouse's lack of attention or agreement as disrespect.
    *   **B. Extreme Emotional Instability:**
        *   They react intensely to setbacks or unmet needs with heightened emotions.
        *   Their mood swings and outbursts create a tense environment.
    *  **C. Controlling Behavior:**
        *   They attempt to control every aspect of their spouse's life.
        *   This includes what they wear, who they socialize with, and their career.
    *   **D. Lack of Accountability:**
        *   They never accept responsibility for their actions or marital issues.
        *   They blame their partner for everything that goes wrong, often using manipulative justifications.
        *   They shift blame rather than acknowledging their errors.
    *   **E. Exploitative and Unempathetic:**
        *   They constantly demand emotional and other forms of support from their spouse.
        *   They fail to offer any support in return, showing a lack of empathy.
        *  They show no compassion to your pain and seeking comfort.
*   **II. Core Traits of NPD:**
     *  **A. Lack of Empathy:**
        * The fundamental inability to understand or share the feelings of another.
     *  **B. Extreme Selfishness:**
        *  A constant focus on one's own needs and desires.

**Core Point:** NPD individuals in marriage are characterized by self-centeredness, emotional volatility, controlling behaviors, a refusal to accept responsibility, and a lack of empathy.

**Fundamental Point:** Identifying the core traits of NPD—a lack of empathy and extreme selfishness—is crucial for recognizing the disorder and making informed decisions, such as ending a relationship with an NPD individual.

**Overarching Framework:** The content outlines the detrimental impact of NPD on marital relationships, highlighting specific patterns of behavior and ultimately focusing on the core traits to watch out for and ultimately protect oneself.
