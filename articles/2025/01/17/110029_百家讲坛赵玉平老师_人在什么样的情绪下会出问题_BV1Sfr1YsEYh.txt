3.  **根本观点 (Fundamental Point):**  了解情绪对决策的影响，能帮助我们避免在情绪波动时做出后悔的决定。

4. **Overarching Framework:** The content explores how specific negative emotional states (sadness, excitement, fatigue) can impair rational decision-making.
