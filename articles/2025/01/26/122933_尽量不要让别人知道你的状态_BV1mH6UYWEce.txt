1.  **Importance of Self-Containment:**
    *   Minimize external expression: Reduce talking and emotional displays.
    *   Maintain a calm and mild demeanor.
    *   Avoid oversharing, especially personal struggles.
    *   External validation is often superficial and unreliable.

2.  **Understanding Human Nature:**
    *   Empathy is rare; most people cannot deeply understand your emotions.
    *   Expect limited genuine support from others.
    *   Self-reliance in managing internal struggles and pain.
    *   Be aware of potential malicious intent from others, even in disguise.
    *   Giving help doesn’t guarantee reciprocity; expect possible mistreatment.

3.  **Controlling Expectations and Managing Emotions:**
    *   Avoid projecting expectations on others to prevent disappointment.
    *   Do not seek validation in return for kindness.
    *   Manage emotions and avoid becoming emotionally dependent on others.
    *   Learn to conceal true feelings and thoughts for protection.

4.  **Importance of Strategic Information Management:**
    *   Conceal your true state and strategies to maintain leverage.
    *   Keep your vulnerabilities and "cards" hidden.
    *   Protect your "bottom line" information.
    *   Recognize that others' perception of you is more important than your actual skill.

5.  **Strategic Communication:**
    *   Carefully manage the information you reveal to others.
    *   Avoid oversharing or exposing your inner thoughts.
    *   Be cautious of any slip-ups, as they can be exploited.
    *   Observe and stay silent when others are negative or gossiping.
    *   Don't feel obligated to engage in every conflict.

6.  **Maintaining Boundaries and Self-Preservation:**
    *   Do not expect to find a deeply empathetic confidant; that’s very rare.
    *   Avoid becoming an emotional dumping ground for others.
    *   Focus on your own issues, not on others’ feelings.
    *   Prioritize self-care and personal well-being.

7.  **Acceptance and Moving Forward:**
    *   Accept reality and focus on self-healing.
    *   Avoid dwelling on negative experiences and people.
    *   Learn to let go and move past the bad experiences.
    *   Don't waste energy on trying to change what cannot be.

**Fundamental Point:**

The fundamental point is that you should manage your emotions and your expectations of others while focusing on self-reliance to build personal strength.

The overarching framework is the concept of self-preservation and strategic interaction in a world where genuine understanding and support are rare. This involves emotional control, limited expression, and strategic information management to gain advantage and avoid exploitation.
