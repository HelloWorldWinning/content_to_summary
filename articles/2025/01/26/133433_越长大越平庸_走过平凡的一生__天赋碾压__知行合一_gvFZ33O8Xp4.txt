*   **A. The Limits of Ability:** Accepting one's limitations and choosing different paths is okay.

*  **C. The Value of Self-Comparison:** It's important to compare with one's own self rather than falling into the trap of comparisons with others.
*   **D.  Living Authentically:** Living a fulfilling life involves pursuing personal passions, rather than simply following the crowd and engaging in empty criticism of others.
