   C.  A life of comfort and ease will prevent any individual to achieve a significant accomplishment.
   D. Overcoming adversity is essential for success.

**IV.  How to avoid being eliminated by society:**

   A. Embrace lifelong learning.
   B. Develop keen awareness of global trends.
   C. Adopt a long-term vision.
   D. Cultivate high emotional intelligence and emotion control.
