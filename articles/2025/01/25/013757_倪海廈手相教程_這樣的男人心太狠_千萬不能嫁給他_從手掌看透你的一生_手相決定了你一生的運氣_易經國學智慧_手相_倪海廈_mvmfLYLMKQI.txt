    *   The concept of "名堂" (Ming Tang), meaning a space or "hall" in front of a building, is crucial in Chinese culture and Feng Shui.

*   **手相 (Palm Reading):**
    *   Palm reading can reveal insights into one's character, health, career, family relationships, and financial status.
    *   Different areas of the palm (e.g.,坎卦, 讯卦, 离卦, 坑瓜) are associated with different aspects of life.

    *   Ancient Chinese astronomy (天际道) connects celestial events with earthly occurrences.
    *   The positions of stars like 火星, 太白星, and others can be interpreted to predict future events.
*   **猪位 (Zhu Wei):**
    *   "猪位" is a term relating to the overall assessment of a person's appearance and demeanor, encompassing their physical form and spiritual essence, crucial for accurate fortune-telling.

**IV. Overarching Framework:**

The overarching framework is a traditional Chinese metaphysical view where observable phenomena, whether in the environment (Feng Shui), on the body (palmistry), or in the sky (astronomy), reflect and impact a person's life trajectory.
