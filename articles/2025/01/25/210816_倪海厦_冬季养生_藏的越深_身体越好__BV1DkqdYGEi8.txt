    *   **Early to Bed, Late to Rise (早睡晚起):** In winter, one should go to bed earlier and wake up later, aligning with the concept of conserving energy (收藏) and keeping warm (藏起来).
    *   **Control Emotions (收敛情志):** Avoid excessive emotional swings. Try to keep your emotions calm and collected.

    *   **Contrast with Traditional Belief (与传统观念对比):**  Winter swimming or exposing oneself to ice water may not always be beneficial. Traditional Chinese medicine suggests that cold exposure might induce fever-like conditions (喊起).

    *   **Washing Feet is Sufficient (洗脚即可):** In winter, it is not necessary to take long hot showers. Washing feet with lukewarm water is sufficient.
    *   **Gradual Cold Water Exposure (逐渐适应冷水):** Starting with slightly cold water (not ice water) on the feet can gradually help the body warm up.

**IV. Dietary and Lifestyle Habits for Longevity (长寿的饮食和生活习惯)**
    *   **Simple Diet (饮食简单):** Eat moderately, focusing on a simple and balanced diet. 
    *   **Moderation in Eating (适度饮食):** Do not overeat; aim for 70-80% fullness during normal times and 50% when sick.
    *   **Regular Bowel Movements (规律排便):**  Ensure regular bowel movements and avoid constipation. 

**V.  Over-Inspection (过度检查)**
    *   **Avoid Over-Diagnosis (避免过度诊断):**  Do not be over-eager to investigate minor health issues, or force a diagnosis too soon, like forcing water out of a dry cloth.
