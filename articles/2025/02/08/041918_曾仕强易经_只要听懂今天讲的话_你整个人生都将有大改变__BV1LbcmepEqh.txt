*   **Embrace Uncertainty:** Accept the uncertainty of life to avoid unnecessary suffering and to live more authentically.

**II. Conclusion:**

*   **Core Point:** The core is to prioritize continuous self-growth while focusing on effort rather than being fixated on results.
*   **Fundamental Point:** The fundamental point is to embrace the inherent uncertainty of life, allowing for a more fulfilling and less stressful existence.
