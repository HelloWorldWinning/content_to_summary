**Outline and Structured Summary:**

*   **I. The Nature of Hoovering in NPD Relationships:**
    *   回袭 (Hoovering) is a particularly dangerous phase in NPD relationships, often underestimated.
    *   Unlike genuine reconciliation, NPD hoovering lacks sincerity and is manipulative. Observe their tactics to understand their exploitative nature.
*   **II. Tactics and Motivation Behind Hoovering:**
    *   NPDs employ a combination of strategies: initially trying to charm and appeal to your emotions.
    *   If charm fails, they resort to threats, using children or flying monkeys (飞火) as leverage.
    *   The core issue is that NPDs treat you as a tool, disregarding your individuality and boundaries.
*   **III. The Damaging Impact of Hoovering:**
    *   Hoovering creates a "broken window effect," further depleting the victim's already diminished emotional resources.
    *   It can lead to despair and an inability to escape the toxic relationship.
    *   Victims often underestimate the extent to which NPDs will exploit their weaknesses.
*   **IV. Why Hoovering is Effective:**
    *   The previous breakup was often ambiguous, leaving lingering questions and a desire for closure.
    *   NPDs reappear as if nothing happened, seemingly oblivious to past events, which is disorienting.
    *   It's likened to a predator stalking prey: NPDs are patient, control their emotions, and adapt their behavior to achieve their goal.
*   **V. The Victim's Dilemma:**
    *   When the NPD reappears, the victim is torn between seeking answers and fearing a return to the toxic relationship.
    *   Any sign of weakness makes the victim vulnerable to being pulled back in.
*   **VI. Importance of Resisting Hoovering:**
    *   Resisting hoovering is crucial for healing and recovery from the NPD relationship. It must be a priority.

**Core Point:**

NPD 回袭 (Hoovering) is a manipulative tactic used by NPDs to lure victims back into toxic relationships by exploiting their emotional vulnerabilities and needs for closure, making resistance a vital step in healing.
