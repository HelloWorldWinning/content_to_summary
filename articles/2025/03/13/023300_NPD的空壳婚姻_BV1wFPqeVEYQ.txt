## Summary of Marriages with NPD (Narcissistic Personality Disorder)

**1. Outline:**

*   **Problem:** Marriages with NPD individuals are described as nightmares devoid of happiness, characterized by constant conflict, devaluation, and emotional abuse.
*   **NPD's Motivation for Marriage:** NPD individuals enter marriage with ulterior motives, seeking someone easily manipulated and controlled to serve their needs and bolster their ego.
*   **Dual Persona:** NPD individuals present a charming and considerate facade to the outside world while displaying cruelty and contempt towards their family at home.
*   **Lack of Empathy and Support:** NPD spouses lack empathy and provide no emotional support, treating their family as servants and extensions of themselves.
*   **Distorted Reality:** Over time, partners of NPD individuals lose sight of what a healthy marriage should be, becoming isolated and emotionally numb.
*   **Control and Isolation:** NPD individuals actively sever their partner's connections with friends and family, further isolating and controlling them.
*   **Final State:** Victims become emotionally and practically dependent, feeling trapped and devoid of agency.

**2. Core Point:** Marriages with individuals suffering from NPD are inherently dysfunctional and abusive, designed to control, isolate, and emotionally destroy their partners for the NPD individual's self-serving purposes.

**3. Overarching Framework:**

The framework describes the progression of an NPD marriage, from the initial manipulative selection of a partner to the eventual isolation and emotional annihilation of the victim. It highlights the stark contrast between the NPD individual's public persona and their private behavior, as well as the gradual erosion of the victim's self-worth and independence.
