## Summary of Ear Acupuncture Points:

**1. Core Point:**  Acupuncture at specific ear points can treat various ear problems like inflammation, ear pain, and tinnitus by directly accessing the internal ear structures.

**2. Overarching Framework:**

The framework is based on using specific ear acupuncture points (耳门, 听宫, 听会) related to *骨凤*, and a specific blood vessel(血道) to treat ear conditions.  It also covers diagnosis based on ear discharge color and methods to keep the ear dry using traditional Chinese medicine remedies (e.g., 凡士林 and 麝香).

**3. Outline:**

*   **Ear Anatomy & Key Points:**
    *   **耳屏 (Ear Bottle):** Structure resembling three peaks.
    *   **骨凤 (Bone Phoenix):** Located in front of the ear bottle, opens when the mouth opens.
    *   **耳门 (Ear Gate):** Above the Bone Phoenix.
    *   **听宫 (Hearing Palace):** Middle point, where three 长军 (long soldiers) related to the gallbladder meridian enter.
    *   **听会 (Hearing Convergence):** Below the Hearing Palace.
*   **Treatment Applications:**
    *   These points (耳门, 听宫, 听会) can be used to treat ear inflammation, ear pain, and tinnitus.
*   **Acupuncture Technique at 听宫:**
    *   Needle insertion goes directly into the 听宫 point, up to one inch deep.
    *   Minimal bleeding due to lack of major blood vessels.
    *   Needle manipulation: Can be used with a flat reinforcing or reducing technique.
*   **Diagnosis from Ear Discharge:**
    *   Yellow discharge indicates a heat pattern (热证).
*   **Post-Treatment Care & Remedies for Drying the Ear:**
    *   Use drying agents like 凡士林 or 麝香 to keep the ear dry, which prevents inflammation.
    *   麝香 is particularly effective at penetrating and clearing blockages.
    *   Formula: 麝香 + 纪锤 + 凡士林 for drying.
